How is the Annual Fund Fee calculated?

The Annual Fund Fee is derived by applying a yearly fund fee rate of 2% based on daily Assets Under Management. This fee is calculated daily and withdrawn quarterly to ensure transparent and predictable fee management.

  • The 2% annual rate is applied pro-rata based on the actual number of days in each quarter.
  • Daily AUM is calculated at the end of each trading day.
  • The fee covers fund administration, custody, and operational costs.
